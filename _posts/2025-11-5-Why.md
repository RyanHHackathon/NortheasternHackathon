We're seeing mass public adoption of Smart TVs, and when you go to stores, you'll see that just about every TV for sale is a Smart TV. Behind these smart TV conveniences is a system that quietly collects vast amounts of data and communicates constantly. Many users never see or understand this process, which captures a screenshot of your TV, some even capturing 100 per second. This hackathon aims to target the communication sent back to the company, a process that is ingrained into all smart TVs, allowing students to see the trouble with a product that is in most people's houses.
