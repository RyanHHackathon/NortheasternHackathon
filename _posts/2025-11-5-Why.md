We're seeing mass public adoption of Smart TVs, and when you go to stores, you'll see that just about every TV for sale is a Smart TV. These Smart TV systems often serve as vector points for an incredible amount of data collection on you, and most people aren't aware. On top of that, they're easily hackable, and can be used maliciously by those with ill intentions. This hackathon aims to target a piece of technology that is ingrained into all smart TVs, allowing students to see the trouble with a product that is in most people's houses.
